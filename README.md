Social Networking Platform for College Students <br>

Ncubook is a dedicated social networking platform for college students, offering a safe and engaging space to connect, collaborate, and share. Built exclusively for students, Ncubook ensures authenticity through verified profiles linked to official college email domains. <br>

Key Features: <br>

Verified Profiles: Gain a blue tick by verifying your college email via OTP. Customize email domains in settings for your institution. <br>
Buddy Requests: Send and accept requests to expand your college network. <br>
Private Messaging: Chat directly with peers to discuss projects or just stay connected. <br>
Interactive Feed: Share notes, pictures, and useful links with your classmates and followers. <br>
Student-First Design: Tailored specifically for college students to foster academic and social collaboration. <br>
